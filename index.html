tex-Rex

AI-Powered Academic Paper Analysis Tool

tex-Rex is an AI-powered academic paper analysis tool that helps researchers improve their scientific manuscripts through automated feedback and suggestions. The tool will remain faithful to the original writing of the author but suggests improvement and asks for missing information.
