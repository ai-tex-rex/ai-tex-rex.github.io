tex-Rex: AI-Powered Scientific Writing Assistant

tex-Rex is an AI-powered academic paper analysis tool that helps researchers improve their scientific manuscripts through automated feedback and suggestions. Inspired by MIT's Nuclear Science and Engineering Communication Lab guidelines, it provides comprehensive analysis for each section of your paper.

Installation

Requirements

  • Python 3.10 or higher
  • pip3
  • curl or wget
  • unzip

Quick Install


# Using curl
curl -O https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ai-tex-rex/ai-tex-rex.github.io/main/install.sh && chmod +x install.sh && ./install.sh

# Or using wget
wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ai-tex-rex/ai-tex-rex.github.io/main/install.sh && chmod +x install.sh && ./install.sh

Components

Abstract Analysis

Following MIT's guidelines for effective abstracts, this component evaluates:

  • Comprehensive structural analysis
  • Key components presence and organization
  • Research impact clarity
  • Reader engagement effectiveness

Introduction Review

Based on established scientific writing principles, analyzes:

  • Background information and context
  • Research gap identification
  • Objectives and hypothesis statements
  • Literature review integration

Methods Evaluation

Ensures your methodology is clear and reproducible by checking:

  • Procedure reproducibility
  • Experimental detail completeness
  • Technical accuracy and clarity
  • Statistical approach appropriateness

Results Assessment

Reviews your findings presentation through:

  • Data presentation effectiveness
  • Statistical analysis validity
  • Research question alignment
  • Interpretation clarity

Conclusion Analysis

Evaluates your conclusion's effectiveness by checking:

  • Research objectives alignment
  • Impact statement clarity
  • Future work suggestions
  • Research context placement

Figure Analysis

Ensures your figures effectively communicate your research by evaluating:

  • Scientific figure design principles
  • Visual clarity and effectiveness
  • Caption completeness
  • Data representation accuracy

Usage

tex-Rex is designed to be simple to use while providing comprehensive analysis:


# Initialize analyzer
analyzer = AbstractAnalyzer(api_key="your-openrouter-api-key")

# Analyze text
result = await analyzer.analyze_abstract(your_text)

# Display results
analyzer.display_results(result)

Writing Guidelines

tex-Rex's analysis is based on MIT's NSE Communication Lab guidelines. For detailed writing guidance, we recommend consulting:

  • MIT NSE CommKit
  • Your institution's writing center
  • Your field's style guides

Remember that tex-Rex is a tool to assist your writing process, not replace human judgment and expertise.
